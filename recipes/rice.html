Basmati Rice

Description

This is basmati rice, a great accompanient to most curries and dishes as well as a great side on its own!

This rice here is flavored with spices and aromatics to enhance its flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook the rice in the water.
  2. Add spices
  3. Serve.
