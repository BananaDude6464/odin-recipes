Garlic Naan

Description

Garlic naan is great, warm, and buttery accompanient to any curry or spicy dish that would be enhanced with a some tasty carbs!

The garlic butter really rounds out the flavors of the bread and adds to the savor of it's paired dish.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Naan

Steps

  1. Warm the naan
  2. Heat up the butter
  3. Add the garlic to the butter
  4. Brush the garlic butter onto the naan.
